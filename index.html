Banana Bread

Prep time: 15 minutes

Bake time: 55-60 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 overripe bananas
  • 1/3 cups of unsalted butter, melted
  • 1/2 teaspoon of baking soda
  • 1 pinch of salt
  • 3/4 cup of brown sugar
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 1 + 1/2 cups of all purpose flour
  • 1 cup of chocolate chips (optional)

Kitchen Equipment

  • Oven
  • 8" x 4" loaf pan
  • Measuring cups and spoons
  • Large mixing bowl
  • Fork

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Butter 8" x 4" inch loaf pan.
  3. Mash banannas with a fork in a large mixing bowl untill completely smooth.
  4. Stir in melted butter into the mashed bananas.
  5. Mix in baking soda and salt.
  6. Stir in sugar, beatten egg, and vanilla extract until evenly distributed.
  7. Mix in flour until combined.
  8. Add and stir in chocolate chips, if desired.
  9. Pour batter into loaf pan.
  10. Bake for 55-65 minutes at 350 degrees F, or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
  11. Remove from oven, and let cool for 5 minutes.
  12. Cut, serve, and enjoy!
